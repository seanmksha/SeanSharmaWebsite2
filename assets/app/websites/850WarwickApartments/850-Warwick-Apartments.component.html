850 Warwick Avenue Apartments



850 Warwick Avenue Apartments is a 50-unit apartment complex.



Sean Sharma designed the front-end from scratch. It uses WordPress as a CMS, and a custom theme based off BootStrap made by Sean. The website shows the property to potential renters, and provides them with a renters application. The client has the ability to post newsletters and edit the rules and regulations without having to contact the developer through the CMS.

A networked first person shooter with knock back mechanics. A more indepth summary on the project can be found at: www.polyseum.com along with a trailer.

Teams of 3 face off against eachother in a variety of game modes that are tied to maps. Choosing a weapon before the match is like choosing what position you want to play, and you stick to it for the duration of the match.


Some of my responsibilities were:
  • Implementing general programming gameplay/network code
  • Implementing assists/kills/death tracking and UI logic
  • Sent over C++ Metrics per round and match types to the backend website
  • Implementing new modes such as Areana, VIP Arena, Capture the Flag, and VIP Capture the Flag
  • I helped make the architecture for the entire game at the beginning of the year
